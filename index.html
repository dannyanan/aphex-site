introduction

Aphex is a display typeface constructed of simple triangular and rectangular shapes.

Originally drawn for and inspired by the music of Aphex Twin, Aphex Display takes cues from the electronic artist’s musical subject matter in it’s jagged, angular, linear, and sometimes nearly-illegible letterforms.

Aphex comes in four styles: Black, Outline, Color and Monochrome. Lowercase letters, 0–9 numerals, and punctuation are forthcoming in version 2.
